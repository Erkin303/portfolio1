Maldives for Kids

The Maldives, a blue, paradisical lagoon, are often associated with the pleasures of seaside resorts. However, the daily life of the people contrasts radically with that of its tourists. This disconcerting paradox demands particular attention, especially in the area of Children’s Rights. Five years ago, government efforts were undertaken in the aftermath of a natural catastrophe caused by a tsunami; however, the situation remains worrisome.
